About I.S.C.

Trusted kitchen exhaust cleaning since 1979. Locally owned, professionally operated, and committed to excellence.

Our Story

I.S.C. started cleaning cooking facilities in 1979. It was purchased in 1990 by Steve Gantz, who has remained the president of the company ever since.

Over the years, I.S.C. has cleaned thousands of cooking facilities and has grown to become one of the largest hood and duct cleaning companies in the United States.
